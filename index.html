Bengali Bites Bistro

Unit-H, 230 Dalhousie Dr Winnipeg, MB, Canada, Manitoba

We started with nothing but a dream — a small kitchen, a few trusted recipes from our mothers and grandmothers, and the burning desire to share something special. In a new country, far from the comforting chaos of Bengali streets and the familiar aroma of home kitchens, we opened our doors not just to serve food — but to share a piece of our soul.

For those who’ve never tasted Bengali cuisine, we pour our hearts into every dish — to introduce them to the richness, the complexity, and the warmth of a cuisine layered with hundreds of spices and centuries of tradition. It’s food that’s bold yet delicate, fiery yet soulful — a celebration of our culture on every plate.

And for those who’ve left Bengal behind — for the students, the expats, the travelers who miss the sound of mustard seeds popping in hot oil or the scent of freshly made khichuri on a rainy day — we offer home. Here, we recreate the meals that your mother made, the flavors of family gatherings, the comfort of simpler times.

It hasn't been easy — but every smile, every returning guest, every “just like home” has kept us going. What began as a humble dream is now a place where memories are stirred, conversations are shared, and Bengal lives on — far from home, yet close to the heart.

Welcome to our table. Welcome to Bengali Bites Bistro.
